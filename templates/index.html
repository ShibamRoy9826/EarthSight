About

Our planet faces unprecedented environmental challenges from air pollution to carbon emission that contribute to this climate change we also withness the rising temperatures due to pollution which is making it very hard for te people to go outdoors.

This scorching heat even became a reason for the end of many lives. But inorder to solve this global issue the most important is self awareness. And that's why we have created Pollution to Power. This includes tools like a carbon footprint calculator and a locality report tool , which let's you know about the condition of your locality, including the air quality index and major pollutants. Along with all the information, our project also gives you personal suggestions to avoid pollution.
